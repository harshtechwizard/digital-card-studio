# Digital Card Studio - User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to Digital Card Studio! This guide will help you create and manage your professional digital business cards.

## User Journey

### 1. Sign Up / Sign In
- Create your account or sign in to an existing one
- After signing in, you'll be directed to your profile page

### 2. Complete Your Profile
**This is the first and most important step!**

Your profile contains all the information that will be used across your business cards:

- **Personal Information**: Name, email, phone numbers, address, bio, profile photo
- **Education**: Add your degrees and qualifications
- **Professional**: Add your job titles, companies, and work information
- **Showcase**: Add awards, products/services, and photo gallery

**Important**: You must at least add your full name before creating cards.

### 3. Create Business Cards
Once your profile is complete:
- Go to "My Cards" from the navigation
- Click "Create New Card"
- Choose which information to display on each card
- Customize the card name and URL slug
- Save your card

### 4. Share Your Cards
- Each card gets a unique URL (e.g., `yoursite.com/card/your-name`)
- Click "Share" to copy the link
- Share it via email, social media, or messaging apps

### 5. Track Analytics
- View how many people have viewed your cards
- See location data (city-level tracking)
- Monitor engagement over time

## Tips for Success

1. **Complete Your Profile First**: The more information you add, the more professional your cards will look
2. **Create Multiple Cards**: Make different cards for different purposes (networking, job hunting, etc.)
3. **Keep URLs Simple**: Use clean, memorable slugs for your card URLs
4. **Update Regularly**: Keep your information current
5. **Check Analytics**: Monitor which cards perform best

## Navigation Flow

The app is organized logically:
1. **Profile** - Set up your information
2. **My Cards** - Create and manage cards
3. **Analytics** - Track performance

## Need Help?

If you see a banner asking you to complete your profile, click it to go directly to the profile page and add your information.
